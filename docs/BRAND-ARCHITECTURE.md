## 4. Product Line 1: ESG-GRC

### First Market-Ready Application

**Product Name**: TIS-IntelliMat ESG-GRC
**Full Name**: TIS-IntelliMat ESG Governance, Risk & Compliance Platform

**Market Positioning**:
> "The AI-powered ESG-GRC platform that transforms compliance from burden to business advantage"

### Product Description

**What is ESG-GRC?**

TIS-IntelliMat ESG-GRC is an enterprise SaaS platform that helps organizations:
- **Govern**: Establish ESG policies, frameworks, and accountability
- **Assess Risk**: Identify, measure, and mitigate ESG risks across operations and supply chains
- **Ensure Compliance**: Meet regulatory requirements and industry standards with automated reporting

**Powered by AI**:
- 9 specialized AI agents for different ESG domains
- Automated compliance assessments
- Real-time risk monitoring
- Intelligent supplier screening
- AI-generated reports and board briefings

### Key Features

**1. ESG Assessment & Maturity Scoring**
- Comprehensive ESG maturity assessment across E, S, G pillars
- Automated gap analysis against industry benchmarks
- 12-month improvement roadmaps
- AI Agent: ESG Assessor

**2. Supplier Intelligence & Screening**
- Database of 493+ suppliers with ESG scores
- Automated supplier risk assessment
- Compliance violation tracking
- Supply chain ESG analytics
- AI Agent: Supplier Screener

**3. Carbon Accounting & Emissions Management**
- Scope 1, 2, 3 emissions calculation
- Carbon footprint tracking
- Reduction strategy recommendations
- Climate scenario analysis
- AI Agent: Emissions Accountant

**4. Compliance & Assurance**
- Multi-framework support (GRI, SASB, TCFD, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, ISO 50001)
- Automated evidence collection
- Third-party assurance readiness
- Audit trail management
- AI Agent: Assurance Copilot

**5. Energy & Resource Optimization**
- Energy consumption monitoring
- ISO 50001 compliance coaching
- Efficiency improvement recommendations
- Renewable energy planning
- AI Agent: Energy Optimizer

**6. Executive Reporting & Board Briefings**
- Automated ESG report generation
- Board-ready briefing documents
- Stakeholder communication materials
- Real-time dashboards
- AI Agent: Board Briefing Bot

**7. Standards Mapping & Regulatory Intelligence**
- Cross-framework mapping
- Regulatory update alerts
- Compliance requirement tracking
- Best practice recommendations
- AI Agent: Standards Mapper

**8. Transaction Security Framework (TSF) Monitoring**
- Financial ESG risk monitoring
- Transaction screening
- Compliance validation
- AI Agent: TSF Watch

### Target Customers

**Primary Market**: Mid to large enterprises (500+ employees)

**Industry Verticals**:
- Mining & Resources
