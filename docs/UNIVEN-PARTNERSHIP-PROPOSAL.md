## Executive Summary

**TIS Holdings Pty Ltd** proposes a strategic partnership with the **University of Venda (UNIVEN)** to deploy the **TIS-IntelliMat AI-Powered Learning Platform**, providing cutting-edge education in sustainable agriculture, climate action, and ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) to UNIVEN students, staff, and surrounding rural communities.

### Partnership Vision

> "Empowering Vhembe District and beyond with AI-powered education for sustainable agriculture, climate resilience, and community prosperity"

### Key Outcomes

- **1,000+ students** trained in sustainable agriculture and AI technology (Year 1)
- **500+ smallholder farmers** in Limpopo gain access to modern farming education
- **50+ UNIVEN staff** upskilled in ESG, sustainability, and AI pedagogy
- **10+ rural communities** benefit from mobile learning centers
- **Research collaboration** on AI applications in African agriculture

### Investment Required from UNIVEN

- **Year 1**: R750,000 (~$40,000 USD) - Pilot Phase
- **Years 2-3**: R1.2M per year (~$65,000 USD) - Scale Phase
- **In-kind**: Faculty time, facilities, student researchers

### Return on Investment

- New academic programs attracting students
- International research partnerships
- Community impact and visibility
- Revenue from certificate programs
- Technology transfer to rural communities
- Enhanced university rankings (SDG contributions)

- 80%+ rural population dependent on agriculture
- Climate change impacts (droughts, erratic rainfall)
- Limited access to agricultural extension services
- Youth unemployment (>40%)

## 3. Proposed Programs

### Program 1: BSc Agriculture Technology Track (Credit-Bearing)

**Target**: UNIVEN undergraduate students (Agriculture, Environmental Science, Computer Science)

**Duration**: Full academic year (integrated into existing programs)

**Curriculum**:

**Semester 1: Foundations**
- AGRI401: Sustainable Agriculture Principles (3 credits)
- AGRI402: AI & Data Science for Agriculture (3 credits)
- AGRI403: Climate-Smart Farming (3 credits)

**Semester 2: Applications**
- AGRI404: Precision Agriculture & IoT (3 credits)
- AGRI405: ESG in Agribusiness (3 credits)
- AGRI406: Capstone Project (6 credits)

**Delivery**:
- Blended learning (online + on-campus labs)
- AI tutor support 24/7
- Hands-on projects using UNIVEN research farm
- Industry mentorship from ESG-GRC clients

**Certification**:
- UNIVEN academic credits
- TIS-IntelliMat Agricultural Technology Specialist Certificate

**Student Enrollment**: 100 students per cohort

## 9. Success Stories & Case Studies (Projected)

### Story 1: Tshifhiwa's Transformation

**Before**: Tshifhiwa Mulaudzi, a 28-year-old smallholder farmer in Thohoyandou, struggled with low maize yields and unpredictable income. Limited access to agricultural advice meant he relied on traditional methods that weren't adapting to climate change.

**Intervention**: Enrolled in TIS-IntelliMat Community Farmer Training via WhatsApp bot (Tshivenda). Learned about:
- Soil testing and composting
- Water-efficient irrigation
- Climate-smart crop varieties
- Record keeping and financial planning

**After** (6 months):
- Maize yield increased by 40%
- Water usage reduced by 30%
- Annual income increased from R18,000 to R28,000
- Now mentors 5 other farmers in his community
- Enrolled in UNIVEN's part-time BSc Agriculture program

**Quote**: *"The AI tutor on WhatsApp is like having an extension officer in my pocket. I can ask questions in Tshivenda anytime, and it helps me make better decisions for my farm."*

---

### Story 2: Bringing Innovation to UNIVEN

**Challenge**: UNIVEN's School of Agriculture wanted to modernize its curriculum to prepare students for the future of farming, but lacked resources for cutting-edge technology and AI expertise.

**Solution**: Partnership with TIS-IntelliMat to integrate AI, precision agriculture, and ESG into the BSc Agriculture program.

**Results** (Year 1):
- 100 students enrolled in new AgriTech track
- 15 students secured internships with ESG-GRC corporate clients
- 3 student projects presented at African Agri-Tech Conference
- UNIVEN became first South African university with AI-powered agriculture curriculum
- Attracted R2M in research grants for AI in agriculture

**Faculty Quote** (Dr. Phumudzo Tshikalange, Head of School):
*"This partnership has positioned UNIVEN at the forefront of agricultural education in Africa. Our students are now learning skills that make them employable not just locally, but globally."*

---

### Story 3: Community Impact in Vhembe

**Community**: Hamakuya Village (population 5,000, primarily subsistence farmers)

**Challenge**: High unemployment (>60%), youth migration to cities, climate change impacts (droughts)

**Intervention**: Established mobile learning center with TIS-IntelliMat platform
- 80 farmers enrolled in sustainable agriculture training
- 30 youth trained in AgriTech and ESG careers
- Community learning circles meeting weekly

**Impact** (12 months):
- Average household income increased by 22%
- 12 youth employed in ESG compliance roles (remote work)
- Community vegetable garden established (sustainable practices)
- 65% of farmers adopted at least 3 new sustainable practices
- Village now serves as model for replication in 20+ villages

**Chief's Quote** (Khosi Tshivhase):
*"This program has brought hope back to our village. Our young people see a future here, and our farmers are thriving despite the changing climate."*
